Speakers

Merril Burgett

CTO – Pied Piper

Merril Burgett is a software designer, creator of the Pied Piper app and algorithm,

Ameer Basheer

Founder – Spliceline

Ameer Basheer is an Indian American business executive, the chief executive officer of Spliceline. Basheer began his career as a materials engineer and joined Google as a management executive in 2004.

Irena Carpaccio

COO – Helix

Irena Carpaccio is the COO at Helix Inc. He is a prominent figure in Helix's famed presentations and has been a member of the company's executive team for over 30 years.

Richard Biros

Tech Journalist – SF Chronicles

Richard Biros is a writer, blog publisher, UI designer, and the inventor of the Markdown markup language. Biros is from the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, area.

Jony Ive

Founder – LoveFrom

Sir Jonathan Paul "Jony" Ive, KBE, HonFREng, RDI is a British industrial, product and architectural designer who was the former Chief Design Officer of Apple Inc., and the serving Chancellor of the Royal College of Art in London.

Metin Özer

Design Lead – Zeplin

Metin Özer is an independent graphic designer, writer and teacher in New York City. His job at Zeplin revolves around the relationship between interfaces and the concept of time.

Elon Musk

CEO – Tesla

Elon Reeve Musk FRS is an engineer, industrial designer, and technology entrepreneur. He is a citizen of South Africa, the United States, and Canada.
